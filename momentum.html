Momentum

Role(s): Technical Audio Designer, Audio Designer, Programmer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 3D, Wwise
Jun. 2024 - Oct. 2024



Responsibilities:

  • Sound Design: Spearheaded creation and implementation of 40+ sound effects in Ableton Live and Reaper, resulting in more feedback to the player about their actions and environment
  • Dynamic Ambience: Conceptualized and executed a procedural ambience system in Wwise that adapts to player performance, creating a sense of dread and urgency when the player fails
  • Interactive Audio Systems: Pioneered the integration of dynamic flight and combat sound effects responsive to the player's velocity in Wwise, resulting in a better game feel of the player's power level
  • Vehicle Traffic System: Collaborated with a team of 3 engineers to develop a chaotic traffic system with navmeshes, waypoints, and custom Unity editor tools to evoke a sense of panic and chaos

Momentum is an upcoming superhero action game where you play as a novice superhero still learning how to be a superhero who must save a city from Mutant terrorists.
