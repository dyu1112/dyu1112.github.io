Sorcerer Smackdown

Role(s): Producer, Programmer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 2D, Wwise, Adobe Premiere Pro
Developed for: Student Organization
Jan. 2024 - Apr. 2024
Website



Responsibilities:

  • Gameplay Programming: Deployed a projectile-based, 2-player combat system with an associated quick time event in Unity
  • Trailer Production: Conceptualized, wrote, recorded, and edited a gameplay trailer for Steam, showcasing mechanics and mood
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Supervised and facilitated communication between 4 teams to maintain feasible scope and steady progress toward completion of a semester-long game project
  • Audio Implementation: Implemented 20+ sound assets using Wwise

Use both muscles and magic to beat down your opponents and prove yourself as the mightiest mage in the arena! In Sorcerer Smackdown, joining the fight is simple: grab a friend, choose a character, and let the match begin! Unleash both physical and magical attacks using the keyboard for commands, mixing and matching moves to craft devasting combos to prevail against your opponent!
