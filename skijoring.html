Skijoring

Role(s): Audio Designer
Technologies Used: Unreal Engine 5, Wwise, Reaper
Jun. 2024 - Present



Responsibilities:

  • Sound Design: Established a sonic identity and designed 30+ sound effects using Reaper through synthesis, foley recording, and sound processing
  • Technical Audio Design: Spearheaded the implementation of all 60+ SFX, music, and VO assets using Wwise and Unreal Blueprints
  • Mixing: Developed a clean mix in Wwise through sidechaining and speaker routing to improve audio-visual communication
  • Adaptive Music: Implemented music layering and filtering using Wwise and Unreal Blueprints that adapts to the player state, creating a dynamic and fast-paced experience

Skijoring is an upcoming 3rd person split screen co-op arcade racer, where one player controls a snowmobile, and the other controls a skier attached to the snowmobile by a cable. Together they must descend the slopes as fast as they can to out-skijor the impending avalanche.
