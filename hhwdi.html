Ho Ho Who Did It?

Role(s): Audio Designer
Technologies Used: Ableton Live
Nov. 2021
Website
Soundtrack



Responsibilities:

  • Composed the entire soundtrack, consisting of 7 tracks total
  • Reduced the workload of artists and programmers by suggesting the use of non-square puzzle shapes
  • Created sound effects for pushing and pulling puzzle pieces
  • Created sound effects for the player walk cycle

Entry to the MEGA Newbies and Vets Game Jam 2022 with the theme requirement: "Restore." Awarded "Best Audio." Santa Claus has been murdered and the player must restore 3 pieces of evidence presented in the form of jigsaw-like puzzles to uncover Santa's murderer.
