Syncon

Role(s): Programmer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 2D
Developed for: Academic Course
Apr. 2023
Play on your browser



Responsibilities:

  • Telemetry: Integrated user-facing data collection and visualization through automatically generated scatter plot graphs in Unity
  • Gameplay Programming: Implemented basic rhythm game mechanics by tracking the player's inter-onset interval between inputs over time to determine their ability to follow the tempo

Created for PSYC-215: Music, Mind, and the Brain. Syncon is a rhythm game inspired by music psychology research on synchronization-continuation: the ability of an individual to continue entraining to a rhythm after the initial external stimulus is removed. Keep tapping to the beat, but beware as the beat will go away with or without warning twice per level.
