Liminal Pop

Role(s): Technical Sound Designer, Programmer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 3D, Wwise, Ableton Live
Developed for: Game Jam
Jan. 2025
Website



Responsibilities:

  • Audio Implementation: Designed 15+ sound effects and spearheaded the implementation of 30+ audio assets into Unity using Wwise and C# scripting under a strict 2-day time limit
  • Dynamic Audio Systems: Implemented adaptive breathing and ambience audio systems in Wwise responsive to the player’s stress and progression, thereby enhancing the game’s anxious tone

Entry to the 2025 Global Game Jam at USC Games, with the theme requirement: "Bubble." Liminal Pop is a short surreal first person 3D liminal horror game about the cyclical balance between motivation and struggle. In this game, the player must escape from 3 liminal spaces representing 3 distinct mental states: anxiety, aimlessness, and comfort.
