Gumshoot

Role(s): Gameplay Programmer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 2D
Aug. 2024 - Dec. 2024
Play on your browser



Responsibilities:

  • Core Gameplay: Implemented core gameplay features and all of their interactions with the environment in C# and Unity 2D
  • Level Design: Designed 2 levels tutorializing gameplay mechanics in Unity 2D, resulting in a 20% increase in player comprehension and 10% improvement in average play time

Created for CSCI 526: Advanced Mobile Devices and Game Consoles. Gumshoot is a 2D platformer in which the player shoots strands of gum to pull themselves toward surfaces, grab and throw objects, and stun and control enemies.
