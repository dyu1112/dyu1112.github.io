Try Not To Hit [An Unspecified] Bridge

Role(s): Programmer, Artist
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 3D, Autodesk Maya
Mar. 2024
Website



Responsibilities:

  • Gameplay Programming: Spearheaded development and execution of 3D drag and drop functionality that retains objects' collision detection on a moving platform in Unity
  • 3D Modeling and UI: Led production of all 3D and 2D art assets and implemented all menus and UI functionality

Entry to the MEGA April Fools Game Jam 2024 with the theme requirement: "Oh No." Awarded "Best Overall" and "Best Game Design." Your cargo ship has run out of power and is headed straight toward [an unspecified] Bridge! Use your mouse to move the cargo on board to control the ship's balance and steer it in the correct direction. Beware of birds flying around trying to snatch your cargo! Smack birds with cargo to ward them off. Be careful! If the ship is too unbalanced, it may capsize.
