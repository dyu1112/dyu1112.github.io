Role(s): Composer, Programmer, UI
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 2D, Ableton Live
Developed for: Game Jam
Nov. 2022
Website
Soundtrack



Responsibilities:

  • Music Composition: Composed and integrated 4 music tracks
  • Music Switch: Implemented a toggle that switches between 8-bit and non-8-bit soundtracks seamlessly
  • User Interface and Experience: Deployed a fully functional HUD and all menus, including Lives and Score systems

Entry to the Open Alpha Open Game Jam 2022 with the theme requirement: "Mini." Awarded "Best Sound," "Best Design," and "Best Mechanics." In the game, you play as Viri the virus. You can infect cells by entering their nuclei to take control of them to destroy other cells. The objective is to destroy as many cells as you can and survive as long as you can.
