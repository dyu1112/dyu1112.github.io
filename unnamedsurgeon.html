Unnamed Surgeon Game

Role(s): Programmer, Sound Designer
Technologies Used: C#, Unity 3D
Developed for: Game Jam
Jan. 2024
Website



Responsibilities:

  • Gameplay Programming: Took initiative to implement all core mechanics and item interactions utilizing Unity C# scripting in a fast-paced, limited-time environment
  • Sound Design: Recorded and implemented 20+ voiceovers for utilization as humorous sound effects

Entry to the 2024 Global Game Jam at USC Games, with the theme requirement: "Make Me Laugh." In this game, the player needs to find surgical instruments that make funny sounds to keep the light on. If the light goes off, the player loses a life. The player loses when they run out of lives or the light remains off for too long.
